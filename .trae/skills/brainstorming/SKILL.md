# Brainstorming

Refine goals and constraints through questions, explore multiple design options, and present a concise, sectioned design for validation and iteration.

## Triggers
- You need to clarify requirements or choose an approach.
- Early phase before any implementation or tests.

## Guidance
- Ask Socratic questions to uncover assumptions and risks.
- Propose 2–3 viable designs with trade-offs.
- Capture decisions in a design document.

## Output
- A short, readable design document suitable for sign-off.
